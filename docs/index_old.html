The Best Way to Play the Game

The Esports Amateur League is a structured, competitive, amateur league available to players of all skill levels. 

Currently we only support League of Legends. 

You can join solo or with friends. Teammate requests are honored unless skill differences require placement in different divisions. 

League is a team game and is best experienced in a stable team context. We have worked for more than a year with our community to create the best structure for fun and improvement.

Learn the Team Game

League is best played with a stable team over a period of time. This structure changes everything. Pick/ban strategy, scouting and learning to properly communicate on the Rift are skills you will learn that cannot be learned in solo/flex q.



Coaching/Broadcasting

If you would like to try casting or coaching you can join our casting track and/or coaching track. 



Focus Improve

The Esports Amateur League is focused on team and individual improvement. There are no elaborate prizes for winning teams as plenty of tournaments offer this type of incentive. Flaming and being a bad teammate are about the only things that bring about League Office discipline. 

Get to Know The Community


The Esports Amateur League community includes our members, Player Advocates and League Front-Office personnel. While the front-office handles league rules and discipline, Player Advocates are focused on helping members achieve their personal goals.
