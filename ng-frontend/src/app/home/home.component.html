NOT JUST FOR THE PROS

We understand the frustration of watching pros play a deeply strategic team-game and enjoy meaningful relationships while you try to solo-carry every game.

  • Nobody should have to experience toxic teammates and trolls.
  • Without a shared strategy, common goals and a communication plan, gaming is frustrating and isolating.
  • Everyone deserves to play the best version of the game. Now you can!
Start a meaningful esports journey now!

OUR VALUES

Players first.

From team placement to scheduling to league discipline, we try very hard to help you accomplish your individual goals and respect your level of commitment.

Professional Experience.

We aren't all pros, nor do we all aspire to be pros. But that doesn't mean we can't be treated like professionals.

The League office is run in a professional manner. Our Player Advocates are trained in professional attributes and our tools were first tested on professional teams.

Listen Well.

Since the start of the EDL we have made significant changes in response to the community. While we think we are in the right spot, we remain committed to listening and improving based on your feedback.

How It Works

Starting your personal competitive esports journey is simple:

Start your meaningful esports journey now!
1

TELL US ABOUT YOU

Select your sport and age group. We have adult (18+) and Youth Leagues.

2

Enter payment details.

EDL fees for adult leagues are $25 per split ($35 for Youth Leagues) or $170/$238 (15% discount) for a yearly plan (8 splits)

3

Get Team Assignment

Remember, you can sign up solo or with requested teammates. Team assignment happens 1 week before split begins.

4

Prepare for the Split

Get to know your team. Agree on strategy with your team (Player Advocates are available to help with this). Schedule matches (we have a flexible scheduling system to optimize availability for all players)

5

Play, Learn, Improve, Lose, Win and Have Fun!

1 official match per week. Each match is 2 games played back-to-back on scheduled date/time.
